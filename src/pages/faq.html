Frequently asked questions

Quick answers. If your question isn't here, send a note and we'll add it.

Who do you work with?

Small and midsized businesses (SMBs) across Upstate South Carolina and the broader Southeast. Our deepest verticals are property management, builders, real estate, and farms, but most SMB tech problems rhyme.

We do not work on residential or home-network projects.

Do you replace our existing IT person or MSP?

Either, depending on what fits. We offer fully managed IT for businesses without internal IT, and co-managed engagements where we work alongside an existing internal team or MSP and bring the security and platform depth they're missing.

Are you a Microsoft shop or a Google shop?

Both. We've shipped real work on Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace, and we manage endpoints across Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android. We don't pitch a platform switch unless it's actually the right call.

What does an engagement actually look like?

Most start with a 30-minute discovery call. From there, we scope either a fixed-fee project (e.g., DMARC remediation, Intune rollout, a security audit) or a monthly managed/co-managed retainer. Pricing is transparent and quoted before any work starts.

Are you certified?

Yes. Cody holds the CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional), the most widely recognized senior credential in information security.

How do you handle our data?

We treat client data the way we'd want a vendor to treat ours: scoped access, MFA-enforced everywhere, and short retention windows by default. We never share client data with other clients, partners, or marketing tools.

Do you do on-site work or only remote?

Both. Most work is remote. On-site visits are routine for clients in the Upstate of South Carolina, and available by arrangement throughout the Carolinas and Georgia.

What's the Free IT Security Scan, exactly?

A read-only, vendor-neutral external check of your email authentication (DMARC/SPF/DKIM), exposed credentials in breach data, and public misconfigurations. No software to install. Results in your inbox within one business day. Request one here.

What is GTM Stacks?

GTM Stacks is our fixed-scope foundation engagement for startups, spinouts, and remote-first teams who want a connected, automated, secure tech stack on day one — built to scale into SOC 2, ISO 27001, or HIPAA without re-platforming. Full details on the GTM Stacks site ↗.

Will you sign an NDA?

Yes. We expect to. We also keep one of our own ready if you'd prefer to start there.

What's something you won't do?

We won't recommend a product because we're a reseller. We won't take a project where we don't think we can deliver real value. We won't sell you AI when the right answer is a checklist and ten minutes of training. We will tell you so directly.

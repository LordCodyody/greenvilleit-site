Refund Policy

How refunds and cancellations work for our engagements.

Last updated: {{year}}

Discovery calls and scans

Discovery calls and the Free IT Security Scan are no-cost. No refund applies because nothing was paid.

Fixed-fee projects

Project work is quoted at a fixed fee with deliverables stated in the engagement agreement. If we cannot deliver the work as agreed for reasons within our control, any portion of paid fees attributable to the undelivered work will be refunded. If the engagement is canceled by you after work has begun, fees for work completed up to the cancellation are non-refundable.

Monthly retainer (managed and co-managed IT)

Retainers are billed monthly in advance and may be canceled with 30 days' written notice. Unused portions of the final month are not refundable. We don't lock clients into long-term contracts.

Third-party costs

Pass-through costs for third-party services (software licenses, hardware, vendor fees) are non-refundable once incurred on your behalf.

How to request a refund

Email {{email}} with the engagement reference and the reason for the request. We respond within five business days.

Privacy Policy

How we handle personal information collected through this site and our engagements.

Last updated: {{year}}

1. Who we are

Greenville IT Consulting LLC ("we," "us," "our") is an IT consulting practice based in Greenville, South Carolina. You can reach us at {{email}}.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what we need to provide our services and respond to your requests.

  • Information you give us directly: name, email, business name, domain, and message content when you fill out a form, request a scan, or email us.
  • Information from your scan submission: public DNS records, breach-data lookups against your supplied domain, and publicly available external configuration.
  • Engagement information: data necessary to perform IT consulting work, only as required by the specific engagement and authorized by you.

3. What we don't collect

  • We don't use third-party advertising trackers or marketing pixels on this site.
  • We don't sell your information to anyone.
  • We don't share information you submit with other clients or partners.

4. How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry or fulfill your scan request
  • To deliver the services you've engaged us to provide
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. How we protect information

We treat client data the way we'd want a vendor to treat ours: MFA enforced, role-based access, short retention windows by default, and encryption in transit and at rest where we control the storage. Engagement-specific data handling is documented in the service agreement.

6. Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics (e.g., Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand aggregate site traffic. These do not identify individual visitors.

7. Your rights

You can request a copy of personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it by emailing {{email}}. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Updates to this policy

We will update this page with the date of the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to active clients.

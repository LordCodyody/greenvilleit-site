FAQ

About the scan

Is this actually free? What's the catch?

Yes, it's free, and there's no catch. We get to demonstrate what working with us looks like, and you get a clear picture of where you stand. Most scans don't turn into a project, and that's fine.

Does this touch any of our systems?

No. The scan is entirely read-only and uses publicly available information plus public breach data. We don't install software, don't log into anything, and don't ask for admin access.

Who sees my results?

Just you. We don't publish results, share them with vendors, or feed them into any marketing system. If you decide not to engage with us, we delete them on request.

How is this different from a vendor's free assessment?

A vendor's free assessment is a sales tool — they're looking for an excuse to put their product in front of you. Ours is vendor-neutral. If the fix is a setting change you can make yourself, we'll tell you exactly that.

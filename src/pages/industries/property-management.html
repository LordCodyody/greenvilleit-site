The technology problems we hear most

  • Tenant PII sitting in shared inboxes and ad-hoc spreadsheets
  • Owner reports going out through unsecured email
  • Different software for accounting, maintenance, and lease management, none of them talking to each other
  • Wi-Fi at the property office that drops every afternoon
  • Staff turnover that means orphaned accounts and shared logins

What we do for property managers

Secure the core

  • Email authentication so vendors and tenants can trust your domain
  • Identity hardening with MFA and conditional access
  • Endpoint management on staff laptops and shared office machines
  • Tight onboarding/offboarding so departing staff don't keep access

Stitch the systems together

  • Automate the handoffs between property management software, accounting, and maintenance ticketing
  • Owner reports delivered through secure channels, not as PDF attachments
  • Centralized document storage with permissions that match the org chart

Property infrastructure

  • Wi-Fi survey and remediation at the leasing office

  • Failover so the office stays online when the ISP doesn't

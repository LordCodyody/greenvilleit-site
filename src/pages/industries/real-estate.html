IT for commercial and residential real estate.

Real estate is one of the most-targeted industries for wire fraud and business email compromise. We focus where it matters: securing the email, the documents, and the mobile workflows agents actually live in.

Why real estate is a target

High-value transactions, time pressure, and email-mediated wire instructions make real estate one of the highest-loss verticals for business email compromise. Attackers spoof agents, brokers, title companies, and lenders to redirect closing wires. The fix is unglamorous but works: lock down email authentication, harden identity, and train staff to recognize the specific patterns.

What builders need from IT

Construction technology lives in two worlds: the back-office workflow (accounting, contracts, plans, change orders) and the jobsite (devices, drawings on tablets, vendor coordination). Most generic MSPs handle the back office and ignore the jobsite. We work on both.

Back office

  • Email authentication so subs and suppliers can trust invoices from your domain
  • Document management that keeps current drawings current
  • Secure file delivery to subs, owners, and inspectors
  • Identity hardening so a stolen laptop isn't a project crisis
  • Automated workflows for RFIs, submittals, and change orders

Jobsite & field

  • Tablet device management with current plans synced offline
  • Reliable connectivity at the trailer, including failover
  • Photo and field-note pipelines that don't depend on a single super's phone
  • Secure access to project documents from the field

eDiscovery and litigation readiness

Builders end up in disputes. We've helped builders use Microsoft Purview eDiscovery to respond to legal hold and production requests in a fraction of the time it would take with manual mailbox searches. The infrastructure to make this easy needs to exist before you need it.

The center of our practice. Most of our clients are SMB owners and operators across the Southeast who want senior-level security and platform work without the overhead of an enterprise MSP.

What SMBs actually need from IT

Most SMBs don't need a 24/7 security operations center. They need a few specific things done well: their email locked down so they aren't spoofed, their identity stack hardened with MFA and conditional access, their endpoints managed and patched, and the busywork automated so the team can focus.

That's the work. We do it on Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace, across Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android, and we don't pad the scope.
